Microsoft Typography

Claude Betrisey contributed significantly to text rendering technologies at Microsoft, including the development of the TrueType rasterizer, integration of ClearType, and leadership in the Windows Text Team. His work impacted GDI+, WPF, Silverlight, and XAML, and he is recognized for his expertise in digital typography, font technology, and text rendering infrastructure.

  • Development of the TrueType rasterizer and font production tools
  • Integration of ClearType text rendering into Windows
  • Leadership in Windows Text Team, GDI+, WPF, Silverlight, and XAML
  • Expertise in digital typography and font technology
